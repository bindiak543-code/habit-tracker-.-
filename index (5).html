Surya Sen Mahavidyalaya BSc Mathematics (Hons)

I'm Bindia Kumari Thakur, a Mathematics Honours student currently in my 4th Semester at Surya Sen Mahavidyalaya. Numbers aren't just symbols to me — they're the grammar of the universe.

My passion sits at the intersection of logical structure and clean design. Just as a well-formed proof leaves nothing to chance, a well-designed interface leaves nothing to confusion. I believe the elegance of mathematics and the clarity of good UX speak the same language.

Beyond coursework, I build tools that solve real problems, explore the aesthetics of data, and help others understand complex concepts through patient, structured teaching.
